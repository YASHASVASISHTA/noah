About

With over 8 years of experience in the design industry, I specialize in creating visually stunning and user-friendly digital experiences. My journey began in New York where I studied Visual Arts, and since then I've worked with clients ranging from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

My approach combines aesthetic sensibility with technical expertise to deliver designs that are not only beautiful but also highly functional. When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new coffee shops or hiking in the mountains.
